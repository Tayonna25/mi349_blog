Do you want to be Happy? Does money make you happy?

I think everyone wants to be happy. It seems like everyone is chasing something whether that be a dream, a job, a family, money, or experiences. It all circles back to what is going to make them the happiest or the most comfortable for most. Many social scientists have tried to answer the question: can money make you happy but it has all come down to how you measure happiness and how you define happiness. In a study at University of Michigan when the makings a year started at 100k per year the response of not too happy became non-existent with people making less than 10k per year at 21% not too happy. On a wall street journal from the pre-purchase forecast, people thought happiness would last longer if they bought a material than have been wanting over an experience longer but later in the study it was found a purchase made on experience last way longer. It was also found that buying someone else a gift rather than something for yourself made people happier. The effects of income have not been linear.
